Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI photo meal logging work?

Take a photo of your meal and our AI identifies the foods and estimates calories, protein, carbs, and fat. Review the results, adjust if needed, and log with one tap.

How accurate is the AI?

Our AI provides solid estimates for most common meals. Accuracy depends on lighting and food visibility. You can always adjust portions and ingredients before logging.

Is VitaCal really designed for women?

Yes. VitaCal's approach focuses on supportive, guilt-free tracking. No shaming, no extreme calorie limits. It's built to help you develop a healthy relationship with food.

Are my meal photos stored?

No. Your photos are analysed by AI and immediately discarded. They are never stored on our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play. Cancel anytime from your device's subscription settings -- no questions asked.

Does VitaCal work without a subscription?

Absolutely. The free plan includes 5 AI analyses per week, unlimited manual logging, calorie/macro tracking, and water tracking. Subscriptions unlock more AI analyses.
