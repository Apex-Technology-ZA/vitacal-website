Why Choose VitaCal Over Yazio?

AI Photo Logging on Free

Yazio locks AI photo meal logging behind its Pro subscription. VitaCal includes 5 AI photo analyses per week on the free plan. You can try the core feature without paying anything.

Lower Price Point

Yazio Pro ranges from $7-15/month depending on the plan. VitaCal's Starter plan is $3/month for 15 AI analyses per week -- less than half the cost of Yazio's cheapest paid tier.

Built for Women

Yazio targets everyone. VitaCal is designed for women with a supportive, guilt-free approach. No shaming when you go over your targets. Clear information that helps you make choices that feel right.

Photo Privacy

VitaCal deletes your meal photos immediately after AI analysis. Yazio stores photos on its servers. For users who care about meal photo privacy, this is a significant difference.

When Yazio Might Be Better

Yazio offers intermittent fasting tools, barcode scanning, recipe import, and a large food database. If you practise intermittent fasting or need barcode scanning for packaged foods, Yazio has more built-in tools. Yazio also offers meal plans and recipes as part of its Pro subscription.

The Bottom Line

VitaCal is the better choice for women who want fast AI photo logging at a lower price with strong privacy. Yazio is better for users who want a comprehensive nutrition platform with fasting, recipes, and barcode scanning.
